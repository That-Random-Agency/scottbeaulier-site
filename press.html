Full Bio ~150 words

Scott Beaulier serves as the H.A. (Dave) True Family Dean of the College of Business at the University of Wyoming, an AACSB-accredited R1 public university where he oversees 75 faculty and staff, 1,500 students, and a $12M+ annual budget.

An economist by training, he holds a Ph.D. from George Mason University and has spent more than 15 years in higher education leadership across public research universities, including North Dakota State University, Arizona State University, and Troy University. His record includes growing enrollment during periods of national decline, expanding online programs, securing external resources for faculty and programming, and building partnerships between universities and employers.

Scott is committed to advancing student opportunity, institutional renewal, and regional economic prosperity. He is a frequent source for national media on economics, labor markets, and higher education policy.
