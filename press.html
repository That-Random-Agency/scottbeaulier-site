Full Bio ~150 words

Scott Beaulier serves as the H.A. (Dave) True Family Dean of the College of Business at the University of Wyoming, an AACSB-accredited program serving 1,500 students. An economist by training, he holds a Ph.D. from George Mason University.

Over his decades-long career in higher education leadership — spanning Troy University, Arizona State University, and North Dakota State University — Scott has built a track record of developing business programs that fuse academic rigor with the realities of business in the 21st century. He is an outspoken advocate for making quality education affordable and accessible, and believes the best business schools serve students and the employers who will hire them.

Scott is a frequent commentator on higher education reform, economic freedom, and workforce development. He has contributed to RealClearMarkets, AL.com, and numerous academic journals.
