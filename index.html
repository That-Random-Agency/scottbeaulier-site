About Scott

Scott Beaulier serves as the H.A. (Dave) True Family Dean of the College of Business at the University of Wyoming, an AACSB-accredited R1 institution serving 1,500 students with 75 faculty and staff and a $12M+ annual budget.

An economist by training (Ph.D., George Mason University), he has spent more than 15 years in higher education leadership across public research universities, including North Dakota State University, Arizona State University, and Troy University.

His work centers on advancing student opportunity, strengthening institutional capacity, and connecting higher education with the workforce and economic development needs of the communities he serves.
