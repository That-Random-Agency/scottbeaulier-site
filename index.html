About Scott

Scott Beaulier serves as the H.A. (Dave) True Family Dean of the College of Business at the University of Wyoming — an AACSB-accredited program serving 1,500 students with a $12M+ budget.

An economist by training (Ph.D., George Mason University), Scott has spent over 15 years leading transformative change across public and private universities, from Troy University to Arizona State to North Dakota State.

His approach is uncommon in academia: he spends as much time talking with CEOs and employers as he does with faculty. He believes the best business schools serve students and the businesses that will hire them.
